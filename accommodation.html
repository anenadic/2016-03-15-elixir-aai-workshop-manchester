Travel

The nearest airport is Manchester Airport. It is located about 10 miles (16km) south of the city centre, about 20-30 minutes from the University. The airport has a number of transport links to the city: by train, taxi and bus. For more information please visit the Manchester Airport Web site.

It is easy to travel by train to the University of Manchester due to our proximity to three large rail stations: Piccadilly, Oxford Road and Victoria (a little further away). Manchester's main stations are highlighted in the city map below. For details of timetables, tickets and other rail information, please visit National Rail Web site.
