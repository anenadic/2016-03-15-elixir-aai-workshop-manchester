Workshop Information

This workshop covers the basics of Authentication and Authorisation Infrastructure (AAI), getting started with Shibboleth and setting up Identity Providers (IdPs) and Service Providers (SPs).

The workshop is a mix of lectures and hands-on lessons where participants will have an apportunity to play around with installing and setting up code.

Training materials are based on SWITCH AAI Shibboleth Training - see the Preparation section below.
